Travel Decor

Match, Decorate, and Explore!

Transform global spaces one puzzle at time!

Game Description

Travel Decor is a unique match-3 puzzle game where players combine their love for puzzles with a passion for design and exploration.

Travel across the globe, solve match-3 challenges, and unlock new design opportunities for spaces inspired by famous cities.

From cozy Parisian apartments to luxurious New York penthouses, express your creativity while enjoying captivating gameplay.

How to Play

  1. Swap and match three or more tiles to clear them and earn points.

  2. Complete level objectives such as collecting design pieces or achieving target scores.

  3. Earn rewards to unlock new rooms and decor items.

  4. Decorate each room to reflect iconic styles inspired by global destinations.

  5. Take part in daily challenges for extra rewards and exclusive designs
