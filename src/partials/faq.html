FAQ

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes, Travel Decor can be enjoyed offline, but some features may require an internet connection.

  • Are there in-app purchases?

    Yes, optional in-app purchases are available for boosters and premium decor items.

  • Is the game child-friendly?

    Absolutely! It’s family-friendly and suitable for all ages.

  • How do I sync progress across devices?

    Connect via Google Play or Facebook to sync your progress.

  • How often are new levels added?

    Regular updates bring fresh content, levels, and design challenges.

  • What if I encounter a problem?

    Use the in-game support feature or contact us via the app store.
